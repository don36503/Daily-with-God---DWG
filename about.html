About

Our Story

Founded in 2021, our journey began with a clear vision: to build a brand driven by purpose, excellence, and impact. Established by Daniel Ayomide Oyewolu, the company was created to deliver meaningful solutions while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and innovation.

Based in Lagos State, Nigeria, we operate at the heart of one of Africa’s most dynamic and fast-growing cities. Our location places us at the crossroads of creativity, culture, and opportunity, enabling us to serve a diverse and evolving audience.

Since our inception, we have remained committed to growth, continuous improvement, and creating value for those we serve. As we move forward, our focus stays firmly on innovation, quality, and building lasting relationships.

Daily with God

Daily with God is an expression of this vision—an avenue to listen, learn, connect, and grow with God. Through blogs, devotionals, and other resources, we desire to help people experience God’s grace in their everyday lives and walk closely with Him one day at a time.
